Overview

What is This Research?

This study evaluates four state-of-the-art instance segmentation architectures for their effectiveness in detecting and segmenting vehicle body components, enabling realistic virtual car wrapping simulations.

🚗

Problem

Traditional car wrapping visualization requires manual masking and professional software. AI-driven segmentation can automate this for real-time virtual preview.

🧠

Approach

Four instance segmentation models with two filtering methods are evaluated on a custom car body dataset with 21 car part classes (11 wrappable, 10 unwrappable).

🎨

Output

Binary masks generated from the selected model are used with HSV color transformation to produce photorealistic virtual car wrapping previews.
